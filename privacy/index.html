We use Quick SDK (a third-party integration platform) to simplify integration of analytics, advertising, and attribution SDKs. Quick SDK itself may facilitate data transmission between our application and integrated third-party services.

We may use Google Firebase for analytics, crash reporting, and performance monitoring.
